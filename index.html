Back to the Kitchen

Chef Tom Catherall returns with his newest restaurant in Virginia Highland

the restaurantA little about us.

Tom Catherall, one of the nation’s certified master chefs, has returned to the Atlanta dining scene to open tom tom at 1000 Virginia Avenue, NE in the space formally occupied by Noche in the eclectic Virginia Highland neighborhood. tom tom is classic Catherall with a chef inspired menu that is both innovative and always fresh.


“As a chef I strive to stay informed on what’s trendy, but I will only serve what I think is best. That’s why I plan on serving sustainable fish and meat along with organic vegetables whenever possible. If something is not season then you will not find it on the menu at tom tom. As always I will be serving high quality food and drinks that offer value for the money. That’s my guarantee to you!

Our $20 featuresLittle things make us best in town

Sunday: Sushi

Enjoy our Sushi Sunday featuring 4 pieces of nigiri and 2 special rolls of Chef’s choice.

MONDAY: WHOLE FRIED CHICKEN With MAC & CHEESE

Using the secret recipe he learned while at Cherokee Town Club, Chef Tom updates this dish with organic Springer Mountain Farms Chicken- the Mt. Airy, Ga. - based poultry producer that promises the healthiest, most flavorful and top quality chickens on the market. The whole fried chicken, 8 pieces, is served along side Mac & Cheese whose twist on the dish is a customer favorite.

TUESDAY: PAELLA

Soon to be a new favorite, Chef Tom’s paella features shrimp, calamari, chicken, chorizo sausage and rice from Valencia in a paella pan for two.
